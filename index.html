Hello, I'm Keerthana Raveendra

I'm a student leader and social entrepreneur passionate about creating positive change in mental health and women's wellness. With a strong academic background and diverse extracurricular experiences, I've founded and led multiple nonprofits that address critical social issues.

From presenting at the United Nations to winning business competitions, I combine entrepreneurial thinking with social impact to create meaningful solutions. I'm particularly interested in mental health advocacy, women's health education, and innovative business development.

Currently pursuing my A Levels in Mathematics, Economics, and Business, I balance academics with my commitment to community service and entrepreneurship.

We've hosted mental wellness sessions with professionals in schools and communities, and presented our initiatives at the United Nations Headquarters in New York.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Global Research and Innovation Program

Participated in a three-month program focused on design thinking and developing commercializable products. Collaborated with NUS Singapore to create a double visor prototype with customized eyepower and advanced coatings, addressing a persistent issue for two-wheeled riders who wear both glasses and helmets.

PennSEM Social Entrepreneurship Competition

Named India's First Runner-Up at this prestigious global competition hosted by the University of Pennsylvania. Presented our double visor prototype, competing against 400+ participants from 30+ countries and 160+ institutions.

Junior MBA & Business Pitch Competitions

Developed "Intellidecor," an AI-powered interior design bot, and reached the finals at the IIT Roorkee e-cell business pitch competition. Also created "Aura," a customizable perfume brand, reaching the semifinals at VIT Chennai's TechVilla Business Pitch competition.

Evren - Handmade Clay Accessories Business

Serving as Marketing Head for this small business specializing in handmade clay accessories and home decor items. We sell products both in-person and online, participate in pop-up events, and have generated 11,000 INR in revenue to date.
