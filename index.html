My Nonprofits

Minds Aligned

Founded in 2019, Minds Aligned is a youth-led non-profit organization dedicated to breaking the stigma around mental health and fostering a safe, supportive space for teens and adults to thrive. Our mission is to empower individuals through holistic well-being, human connection, and nature-based healing.

We've hosted mental wellness sessions with professionals in government schools, local communities, and corporate businesses, connecting with individuals across Bangalore and Ahmedabad. Our street awareness campaigns bring psychiatrist-approved knowledge about conditions like OCD, depression, and anxiety directly to the community through engaging conversations in high-traffic areas like Church Street.

One of our innovative initiatives includes puppy yoga sessions, which in our most recent event raised around 13,000 INR while also helping foster puppies get adopted. Minds Aligned had the honor of presenting our mental health initiatives at the prestigious 1M1B Youth Summit at United Nations Headquarters in New York, amplifying our mission on a global platform.

@minds_aligned10

Akshata Foundation

As Co-head of Akshata Foundation, a student-led non-profit organization founded in 2023, I've helped drive our mission to help underprivileged women and children around India by introducing safe sanitary practices and equipment. Our motto, "Nurturing Lifelong Wellness," reflects our belief that everyone has the right to access accurate information about menstruation, cervical cancer, and other common conditions affecting women.

Our key activities include providing access to safe sanitary practices and essential hygiene equipment, delivering accurate, life-saving information about menstruation and cervical cancer, and empowering rural communities through health education programs. To date, we have impacted 452 girls through awareness programs and distributed 400+ hygiene kits via partnerships with 4 government schools in Karnataka.

We've raised INR 40,000+ through fundraising efforts, including bake sales and online platforms. Recently, we were awarded the Netaji Smriti Puraskar 2025 for our contributions to women's health, a recognition that validates our continued efforts to create meaningful change in women's wellness education.

@akshatafoundationofficial

Vital Hope

Founded in the summer of 2024, Vital Hope is a student-led non-profit organization that provides support and assistance to underserved people. As the founder, I've led our initiatives to create tangible impact in vulnerable communities.

Through Project Karnataka, we conducted comprehensive research in Harresamurdha and three neighboring villages, engaging with 44 families to understand their medical conditions and healthcare needs. Our survey revealed a pressing need for a nearby hospital, as access to healthcare remains a significant challenge for the residents. Alarmingly, only one out of the 44 families we spoke to has medical insurance, highlighting the critical gap in healthcare coverage in the area. We are currently planning to organize a medical health camp to address these immediate needs.

Our Project Women initiative successfully raised 30,000 INR to support a street vendor's daughter's education, creating pathways to opportunity for those who might otherwise be overlooked. Through these targeted efforts, Vital Hope continues to identify and address critical needs in our communities.

@vitalhopeorg

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Global Research and Innovation Program

This rigorous three-month program immersed us in design thinking and the development of a commercializable product. We began with seven weeks of intensive online classes, followed by a 10-day visit to NUS Singapore, where we networked with industry experts and collaborated closely with NUS professor, Dr. Tan to finalize our product.

My team focused on a persistent issue for two-wheeled riders who wear both glasses and helmets. We developed a prototype for a double visor featuring customized eyepower and advanced coatings that offer anti-scratch, anti-fog, and glare-reduction properties. This innovative design alleviates pressure from glass temples and significantly reduces fogging, which in turn lowers the risk of accidents.

Our product directly supports SDG 3â€”Good Health and Well-Being, and we are moving forward with patenting our design. In my letter of evaluation, judged by Professor Dr. TAN Wee Kek of NUS, I received 4A*'s and 1 A in commercialisation, reflecting the quality and potential of our innovation.

I was named India's First Runner-Up at the PennSEM Social Entrepreneurship Competition, hosted by the University of Pennsylvania. The competition provided a valuable platform to showcase our innovation to a global audience of social entrepreneurship leaders.

PennSEM is a leading force in social entrepreneurship, known for fostering future changemakers through its high-profile pitch and case competitions. Junior MBA

Through the Junior MBA program, I gained valuable insights into different types of businesses and their operations. My team of four developed "Intellidecor," an AI-powered interior design bot that simplifies the entire interior design process. Users can upload photos of their dream home, and the AI bot visualizes how the space could look, including furniture and decor, while connecting users with trusted contractors and service providers.

This innovative concept led us to participate in the IIT Roorkee e-cell business pitch competition in June 2023, where we reached the finals, competing against high school, college, and MBA students. Our concept reached the semifinals with a top 16% performance, placing us among the top 48 teams out of 300 participants. The experience of developing a business model, conducting market research, and pitching to judges provided practical application of economic principles including market analysis, value proposition development, and financial projections.
